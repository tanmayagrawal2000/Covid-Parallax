Shopping Precautions

  • Try to go out as less as possible for grocery shopping
  • Keep at least 6 feet distance for other shoppers
  • Plan your visit to the nearest grocery shop and already note down the things you need
  • Avoid going at peak hours.
  • Always wear your masks even if you are in your car and its mandatory to wear a mask in public places
  • If you feel you are at high risk then cover your mask with a medical mask.

    • Doctor Visit Precaution

  • Call your doctor’s office to check if telemedicine consultation is available for small concerns like common cold.
  • Avoid crowded settings and maintain appropriate distance from other people
  • Try as much as possible to not go to a hospital because the number of sick people are high which results in greater chances of you contacting someone with COVID19.
  • After returning from doctor’s appointment make sure to take a bath and change your clothes.
